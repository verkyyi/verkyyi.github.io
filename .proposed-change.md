# Proposed Change: evolve-quiet-mode

## Summary
Reduce evolve.yml HUMAN_ACTIVE no-op agent_log entries from every hour to once per 6 hours (hours 00, 06, 12, 18 UTC), cutting ~83% of evolve log contributions.

## Motivation
Evolve writes ~24 agent_log entries per day during HUMAN_ACTIVE mode. Each entry is nearly identical ("no-op, research unchanged, pipeline clear"). This is the dominant driver of agent_log regrowth — agent_log regrew from 30 to 172 lines within 2 hours of truncation (2026-04-13T09:39Z). Reducing to 4 entries/day saves ~20 entries/day (~6KB/day).

## Implementation
Add to evolve.yml prompt: "During HUMAN_ACTIVE mode, only write an agent_log entry on runs at hours 00, 06, 12, 18 UTC. On all other hours, skip the agent_log write entirely. Always log if any action is taken (non-no-op)."

## Risk
LOW — only affects logging frequency during idle mode. All substantive actions still logged. No behavioral change to evolution logic.

## Category
harness improvement (workflow YAML)
